Event Details

Date: November 10, 2024

Time: 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM EST

Location: George Brown College, Casa Loma Campus, Toronto, ON, Canada

Join us for an inspiring "Meet Your Alumni" event as part of the George Brown College Class of 2022 Graduation Ceremony! This special gathering provides the perfect opportunity for graduates to connect with accomplished alumni from various fields, gaining valuable insights into life beyond college. Engage in meaningful conversations, hear firsthand experiences, and learn tips for navigating career paths and future goals. This networking event celebrates the achievements of our graduates and strengthens bonds within the George Brown community. Let's toast to new beginnings, foster connections, and honor the bright futures of our talented Class of 2022.
